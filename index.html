Gabriel Faria's Online Resume

Gabriel Martins Faria completed his Degree in New Communication Technologies in 2018/07 and his master in Multimedia Communication in 2021/02, both from the University of Aveiro. He's currently a PhD student in Information and Communication in Digital Platforms at the University of Aveiro. He works in the area of Social Sciences with an emphasis on Communication and Information Sciences. His research interests are: - Communication; - Evaluation of user experience; - Usability assessment; - Front-end and back-end programming; - Design and development of multiplatform interfaces (web and mobile); - Design for iTV; - Personal assistance software; - 3D modeling and rendering.
